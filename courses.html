Course Reference

About this course reference:

Our members take classes in various departments across the University of Texas to gain insight from other related fields. These courses are extremely valuable, but presently difficult to find as they require knowing a certain class exists prior to checking the official course listing to see if it is being offered during the coming semester. To facilitate the selection of coursework for our members this page contains a listing of every class a current student has taken and their anonymous rating on a scale of 1 to 5. We hope that you find this a useful tool for selecting your future coursework.
