Officers

A message from the board:

We are the Geo-Institute board of officers. We are current geotechnical graduate students who have been elected by the organization members of good standing at the end of the previous academic year. Our goal is to use our time and resources to provide the best graduate school experience to our members during their time at the University of Texas at Austin.
